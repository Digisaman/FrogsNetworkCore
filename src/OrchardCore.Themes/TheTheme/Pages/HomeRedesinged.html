ABOUT US

FROGS is a collaboration platform for inspection and project management consultancy freelancers and companies in the renewable, oil and gas sector worldwide. FROGS is dedicated to freelancing inspection and project management consultancy professionals to showcase profile, share knowledge, exchange technical solutions and explore best practices and find work opportunities. A space to share, inform, inspire and bring the latest news from the field.

WHAT WE DO

We foster collaboration. FROGS is designed to bring together and empower freelancing experts and companies in the field from across the world, and to encourage partnership and collaboration among them.
